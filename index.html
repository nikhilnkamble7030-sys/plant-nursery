About Us

Welcome to Kamble Farm Nursery, nestled behind Shree Yallma Devi Temple on Tanali Road, Kasegaon. Founded in 2022 by the Kamble family, our nursery has grown from a small passion project into a trusted source for premium plants in the region.

At Kamble Farm, we specialize in organic, locally sourced plants that thrive in our climate. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting, our team of experienced horticulturists provides expert advice to help your garden flourish. We pride ourselves on sustainable practices, ensuring every plant is nurtured with care and free from harmful chemicals.

Our mission is simple: we offer plants at affordable prices. Visit us to experience the freshness of our nursery firsthand—we're more than just a shop; we're your partners in green living.
